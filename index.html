About Us

Welcome to TravellU, your ultimate guide to Durga Puja pandal hopping in Kolkata! Our mission is to help you explore the vibrant and diverse cultural heritage of Kolkata through its magnificent Durga Puja celebrations.

At TravellU, we curate a list of the most famous and awe-inspiring pandals, providing detailed information, images, and directions to make your experience unforgettable. Whether you're a local resident or a first-time visitor, our goal is to make your pandal hopping journey smooth and enjoyable.

Join us as we celebrate the spirit of Durga Puja and experience the artistry, devotion, and community that make this festival a true spectacle.
